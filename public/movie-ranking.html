The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

"3 Idiots" is a critically acclaimed Indian comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. It follows the story of three engineering students and their journey to find their true passion in life while navigating the pressures of the education system.

Ye Javani Hai Deewani

"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" is a popular Bollywood romantic comedy-drama film directed by Ayan Mukerji. It features a star-studded cast including Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, and Kalki Koechlin, and tells the story of a group of friends who reunite several years after college and discover the true meaning of love and friendship.

Swadesh

"Swades" is a critically acclaimed Indian drama film directed by Ashutosh Gowariker. It stars Shah Rukh Khan as an Indian-American NASA scientist who returns to his homeland and discovers the harsh realities of rural India, prompting him to take action to bring about positive change in the community. The film is known for its thought-provoking themes and powerful performances.
